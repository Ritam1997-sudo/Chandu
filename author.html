About Ritam Debnath – The Mind Behind FixMyKb

Ritam Debnath

Founder-Author-Developer at FixMyKb

Ritam Debnath is a passionate SEO expert and web developer dedicated to creating user-friendly online tools that simplify everyday digital tasks. With over 1.5 years of experience in SEO and content optimization, he specializes in improving website visibility, enhancing user experience, and developing practical solutions for online audiences.

As the founder of FixMyKb, Ritam has built a platform that offers free, easy-to-use image resizing and compression tools. His expertise in technical SEO, schema markup, and web performance optimization ensures that FixMyKb remains efficient, fast, and accessible to users worldwide.

Beyond SEO, Ritam has a keen interest in content writing, digital marketing, and software development. He believes in data-driven strategies that help websites rank higher on search engines while delivering real value to users. His mission is to make FixMyKb a go-to resource for anyone needing quick and reliable file optimization tools.

When he’s not working on SEO strategies or web development projects, Ritam enjoys learning about the latest advancements in AI, automation, and digital tools.
