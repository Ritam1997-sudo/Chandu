Understanding the Need for Smaller Image Sizes

Several government portals, such as those for applying for an Aadhaar card or submitting a PAN card form, have strict image size requirements. If an image exceeds the limit, it may be rejected, leading to delays. Adjusting the file size to 20KB, 50KB, or 100KB ensures smooth and hassle-free uploads. You can check the official UIDAI guidelines for more details on Aadhaar photo specifications.
