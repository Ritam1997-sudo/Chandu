How to Resize Images to 20KB

Resizing images to a specific file size like 20KB is crucial for applications requiring small, efficient uploads, such as KYC verifications or online forms. This blog explains how to use FixMyKb’s Image Resizer to 20KB effectively.

Why Resize to 20KB?

Small file sizes ensure quick uploads and compliance with strict platform limits. A 20KB image retains enough quality for most purposes while minimizing storage and bandwidth use.

Steps to Use the Tool

  1. Upload Your Image: Drag your JPG or PNG into the drop zone or click to select it.
  2. Choose Automatic or Custom: Click "Resize to 20KB" for a quick 200px-width resize, or input custom dimensions and hit "Custom Resize Image."
  3. Check the Preview: Compare the original and resized images—ensure the size is ≤20KB.
  4. Download: Save your resized image as a JPG with the "Download" button.
