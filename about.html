About Us - FixMyKb

Welcome to FixMyKb, the easiest way to resize images to 20KB, 50KB, or 100KB for Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, KYC verification, and online applications.

We know how frustrating it can be when your image size doesn’t meet official requirements. That’s why we built FixMyKb—a free, secure, and fast tool that resizes images in just one click. No need for complex editing software or paid apps—just upload, resize, and download instantly!

Why Choose FixMyKb?

  • One-click resizing – No technical skills needed
  • Free forever – No hidden costs or sign-ups
  • 100% secure – Your images are never stored
  • Lightning-fast – Resize in seconds

Our Mission

We believe in making digital documentation hassle-free for everyone. Whether you’re applying for a government ID, job, or online verification, FixMyKb ensures your images meet the required size without losing quality.

Our Vision

Our goal is to be the go-to image resizer for official documents, helping users worldwide with a simple, effective, and reliable tool.
